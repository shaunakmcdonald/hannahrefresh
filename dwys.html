Draw What You See,
Draw What You Think You See

Nati Frinj 2015was the host of two ‘Draw What You See, Draw What you Think You See’ sites, which were simply set up with two chairs, drawing materials and a framed view of an iconic view of Natimuk. In this first iteration, the views were Natimuk Creek, and the Natimuk Silos.

Festival goers were invited to sit down, pick up a pen and simply draw, either what they saw, or what they wanted to see.

The results of the drawings can be viewed online via the Nati Frinj Facebook page. To read more about the project (and how it can appear in any festival context), visitwww.madeinnatimuk.com.
