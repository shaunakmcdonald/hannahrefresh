Willaura Lights Up

Over two terms, Hannah worked with the Prep ­ 1, grade 2 ­ 3 and grades 4,5,6 students at Willaura Primary School to create three animated stories. These stories came from older residents of Willaura as they recounted some of their memories of the town, and were captured by The Yarn project held in Willaura in 2015. The three stories­ Hanscombe’s Zoo, The Pictures, and The Rabbits ­ were projected on the side of the school at the end of the school year. Willaura Lights Up was funded by Creative Victoria and the Artist in Schools program.
