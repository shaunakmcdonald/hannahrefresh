Overlaid

Overlaid was a large scale projection installation, which showed over the three nights of Nati Frinj 2015. Part of a larger project titled Rising Lights, Overlaid invited the audience to cast shadows and interact with the three layers of simultaneously projected imagery. Using shadow to disrupt the light, new images were revealed and each new loop was edited together in a different way.
